WHO ARE WE?

Forest Street Carpentry is the product of a friendship that was born in a classroom at the University of Southern Maine. Nolan and Matt bonded over their shared love of skiing. When Nolan was looking for a summer job, Matt set him up with a job. After realizing their foreman would only stifle any creativity they might have, Nolan and Matt decided to strike out on their own. Our philosophy is, if you're exercising your mental and creative capacities, having fun and working in good company, you will be successful.

Nolan Sullivan is a Cape Elizabeth, Maine, native. His talent for carpentry began as a summer job in his teens but has grown to be one of his passions. Nolan is about to finish a degree in environmental policy and planning from the University of Southern Maine. Nolan recently found an interest in art and custom furniture and greatly enjoys the time he spends in the shop. In his free time, you can find Nolan skiing, fishing and hiking.

Matthew Jones grew up in Milford, Maine. Carpentry started as just a job for him, but he has found a way to incorporate his artistic style into the pieces he designs. Matt is about to graduate from the Univeristy of Southern Maine with a degree in environmental studies. When he's not in the shop, Matt snowboards, hikes and spends time with his baby daughter, Lillian Nora.
